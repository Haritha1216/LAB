SVECW, a higher-education institution set up exclusively for women students, provides an excellent opportunity to them for acquiring specific skills and knowledge, fostering their creativity and nurturing their innovative prowess leading to their intellectual development. Established in 2001, Shri Vishnu Engineering College for Women (SVECW) is located in Bhimavaram in Andhra Pradesh. Approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), and affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Kakinada (JNTUK), the College offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various streams of Engineering, Information Technology and Business Management. SVECW is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). The College campus spreads across 100 acres of landscape with a clean climate that provides a congenial atmosphere to pursue higher studies. SVECW provides on-campus facilities such as smart classrooms, Well-equipped library, an indoor auditorium, open-air auditorium, mini auditorium, seminar halls, food courts, Wi-Fi, swimming pool, and fitness centre for the students.
